Applications of Computer Science

Computer Science has many applications in day-to-day life. It is itself defined as the study of the principles and use of computers.
Computer Scientists deal largely with software systems, which includes their theory, design, development, and application.
There is a seemingly endless list to the number of applications, most of which can be derived from other applications. Many major areas include:

  • Artificial intelligence and robotics
  • image processing
  • Speech processing
  • Datamining
  • Visualization and post-process
  • Artificial intelligence and robotics
  • Parallelization
  • Optimization
